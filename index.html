Visualization: Project Two
This visualization overlays data about job postings for soft-drink companies with the rate of adult obesity by state. The soft-drink company data was scraped using Kimono from Monster.com. This data is every job for either Pepsi or Coca-Cola posted in the last month, organized by zip code. The obesity data was accquired by "inspecting element" on a page that had a visualization about obesity data. In the visualization I wanted to differentiate between both the soft-drink type (Pepsi or Coke) and the specific company (Bottling or Manufacturing). I chose two color families to differentiate between Pepsi and Coke and then shades within that family to differentiate between Bottling and Manufacturing. For the chloropleth of obsetiy, I chose a grey scale because this visualization is already very colorful and I thought another color scale coud be distracting. The goal of this visualization was to analyze whether the location of soft-drink companies (which was identified using the job posting location) correlated with the rate of obesity in that location. I believe that this visualization shows that there is a correlation, but that does not indication causation.
Visualization: Project Three
This visualization is showing metro statistical areas color coded by the 2 digit Locus activity of the job with highest location quotient in that area. The location quotient was derived using Bureau of Labor statistics data. For every job in each MSA, I took the (employment of that job in that MSA/total employment in the MSA)/(the employment of that job nationally)/national employment. I then found the job with the highest LQ and removed all other jobs. Once I had the data, I joined the dataset of MSA, LQ, and Locus activity with a polygon shape file of MSAs. I then used the "category" wizard to color code the MSAs. The category wizard only allows for 10 categories, and we had 33 job types to reflect. I used the CartoCSS to add the other categories, but because I didn't want to distinguish between 33 categories, I grouped the jobs by activity and made similar jobs the same color. I then changed the legend to reflect these groups. I also used HTML to change the size and names of the categories. Lastly, I added an info window which lists the MSA name, name of the job, and LQ for each MSA.
